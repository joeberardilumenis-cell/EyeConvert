High-Performance AI Prompts

Copy and paste these prompts into ChatGPT or Claude to simulate negotiations, train staff, and analyze revenue instantly.

The "Objection Destroyer"

Training

Train your staff to handle price objections without hesitation. This prompt turns AI into a skeptical patient that refuses to break character until your staff wins.

"Act as a skeptical patient named 'Karen' sitting in an optometry chair. I am your technician recommending a $1,200 out-of-pocket dry eye treatment (OptiLight). You are resistant because of the cost and because 'eye drops are cheaper.' Do not accept my recommendation easily. Make me explain the VALUE over the cost. If I explain it well, agree. If I hesitate, push back harder. Start by saying: 'That sounds expensive, why can't I just keep using Visine?'"

The "Perfect Analogy" Maker

Patient Ed

Stop using complex medical jargon. This prompt generates custom metaphors based on your patient's profession to make the diagnosis "click" instantly.

"I am an optometrist explaining Meibomian Gland Dysfunction (MGD) to a patient. The patient works as a [INSERT JOB, e.g., Car Mechanic]. Create a simple, powerful analogy using their specific job terminology to explain why their oil glands are clogged and why 'just adding water' (drops) won't fix the engine. Keep it under 3 sentences."

The Profit Modeler

Business

Visualize the ROI of the device. Input your patient volume, and let the AI build a text-based P&L statement for the dry eye clinic.

"Act as a medical practice consultant. I have 2,000 active patients. 20% likely have dry eye. I want to convert 5% of those dry eye patients into a $1,000 premium treatment package this year. Calculate: 1) Total potential revenue. 2) Monthly revenue if spread evenly. 3) How many patients I need to treat per week to hit this goal. Output as a clean table."

The "CFO War Room"

Negotiation

Prepare for the toughest meeting of the year. This prompt creates a hostile CFO who only cares about EBITDA and ROI. Practice your financial defense.

"Act as a strict Hospital CFO. I am a sales rep trying to sell you a $120,000 capital equipment device for the Ophthalmology department. You do not care about 'patient outcomes'—you only care about ROI, payback period, and Section 179 tax implications. Grill me on the numbers. Start by asking: 'Why should I spend 120k on this when we're trying to cut costs?'"

The "Hyper-Personalizer"

Prospecting

Never send a generic email again. Paste a doctor's bio or "About Us" page, and let AI find the perfect hook to start a conversation.

"I need to write a cold email to Dr. [Name]. Here is the text from their 'About Us' page: [PASTE TEXT]. Identify 2 specific things they mention (e.g., a specific technology they use or a philosophy they have) and write a 3-sentence opening hook that connects their philosophy to the concept of 'Restoring Lid Mechanics' without being salesy."

The "Deal Autopsy"

Strategy

Stuck deal? Use this prompt to analyze the situation like a VP of Sales and find the missing piece of the puzzle.

"Act as an expert Enterprise Sales Coach. I have a deal stuck in the 'Verbal Agreement' stage for 3 weeks. The doctor wants it, but the practice administrator has gone silent. Ask me 3 strategic questions to uncover the roadblock, then suggest one specific 'Unsticking Email' to send to the administrator."
